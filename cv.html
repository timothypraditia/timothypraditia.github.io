Curriculum Vitae

Summary

I am a PhD researcher with 10 years of combined industry and academic experience, as well as exposure to intercultural and interdisciplinary environments. I am adaptable and fast to learn new concepts, having started off as a Geoscientist, more specifically Petroleum Engineer, and developed deeper interests in the field of numerical simulation and data science along the way. Since the beginning of my career, I have been using data to identify potential improvement in the cost efficiency of the company's field operations. In my current research, I use data, combined with physics, to improve on existing scientific hypothesis, and ultimately to also aid in better decision making processes. In my free time, I sometimes work on a fun data analytics project in collaboration with fellow Redditors on my favorite football club: Juventus!
