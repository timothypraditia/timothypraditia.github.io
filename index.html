Timothy Praditia

PhD Researcher, University of Stuttgart

Hi there! My name is Timothy, and I am a last year PhD researcher at the University of Stuttgart, under the supervision of Prof. Dr.-Ing. Wolfgang Nowak. My current research focuses on developing physics-informed ANNs for geoscience problems, in particular to identify the appropriate combination of data-driven learning and prior physical knowledge, so that the resulting hybrid model can contribute to a more reliable decision making process. Since I will finish my dissertation very soon, I am looking for jobs in the data science/machine learning research/engineering field. In my free time, I like to travel and take pictures (follow my Instagram ). I am also a huge fan of Juventus (the Italian football club, I even made a small analytics project on the team's performance with fellow Redditors).
